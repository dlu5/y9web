Inquiry and Analysis


Explanation & Justification

Currently the WHO data is displayed in a table that is cluttered and not user-friendly. In order to further educate the public about the legal blood alcohol content (BAC) limit for different countries. Readily available and user friendly data will help reduce drinking and driving related accidents and infringements and further promote safety and responsibility. Among young/novice drivers the legal BAC limit may be hard to find in different countries. Drinking and driving is an issue in society that will not cease to exist until people are educated. Legal BAC limits are often a good reference point for driving sober. Rather than face uncertainty caused by anecdotes or Google searches of inaccurate sources, my site will provided an accurate and user friendly reference for these users. The majority of young/novice drivers do not sort through the WHO website and its cluttered subtopics and sections to scroll through tabular data to find info. My site will provide readily available and aesthetically pleasing data which will prevent law violations and encourage sober driving. HTML5 canvas is a powerful way to graphically display such info.

Design Brief

1. Will have an autocomplete to save the user time when typing in the country name
2. Will have a slider on a number line labeled with the range of legal BAC limits to let the user explore the stats for difference countries
3. Will have a link to WHO stats for users to view tabular data
4. Will have a bar graph of all listed countries and their legal BAC limits to give a quick visual representation
5. Will have option to compare two countries side by side
6. Will have a hover function to show more detailed stats
7. Will have a non-clashing colour theme (preferably blues and greens)

Summary of Analysis

I analysed a variety of well designed websites with up to date data and clean themes and designs. By analysing the best websites, I have been able to glean the best aspects of each to help create a design plan and brief for my site.
