Explanation & Justification

Currently the WHO data is displayed in a table that is cluttered and not user-friendly. In order to further educate the public about the legal blood alcohol content (BAC) limit for different countries. Readily available and user friendly data will help reduce drinking and driving related accidents and infringements and further promote safety and responsibility. Among young/novice drivers the legal BAC limit may be hard to find in different countries. Drinking and driving is an issue in society that will not cease to exist until people are educated. Legal BAC limits are often a good reference point for driving sober. Rather than face uncertainty caused by anecdotes or Google searches of inaccurate sources, my site will provided an accurate and user friendly reference for these users. The majority of young/novice drivers do not sort through the WHO website and its cluttered subtopics and sections to scroll through tabular data to find info. My site will provide readily available and aethetically pleasing data which will prevent law violations and encourage sober driving. HTML5 canvas is a powerful way to graphically display such info.
