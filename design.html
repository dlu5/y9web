Changes to My Design




Please refer to Developing Ideas section of my website for a full list of design specifications and details.

Due to time constraints, I had to make multiple changes to my original plan. I have also added a map, which adds to the user experience. In addition I was unable to CSS the input field with the autocomplete because HTML datalist does not support CSS.


Design Specifcations that were fulfilled:


S1. Non-clashing colour theme (preferably blues and greens and white and black)
S3. Link to WHO stats for users to view raw tabular data
S9. Button changes colour when hovered over and rounded buttons
S10. Faster site loading time (no redundant code and no cluttered pages)
S11. Easy to read text and font (Roboto, pt 12 and up)
S12. Dropdown of countries (the autocomplete displays countries in a "dropdown" style format)
S13. Entry box for typing country with auto-complete
S14. Text sentence of what the legal BAC content is


Changes to my Design Specifications


S2. Hover over plotted data on graph to show more detailed stats

This specification was not fulfilled because I did not include a graph, please see S4 for more details.




S4. Will have a bar graph to display all listed countries and their legal BAC limits side by side to give quick visual representation

I did not include a graph due to lack of time and I have included a map on my site instead. I don't think that a graph would have been very user friendly, as the graph would have too many countries to scroll through.




S5. Favicon for web page

I did not include a favicon due to lack of time, as I deemed it a specification that was of lesser importance to the functionality of my site. This feature could be included in a future release.




S6. Clicking logo or button will bring you back to home page

I attempted to include a logo in the menu bar using Font Awesome Icons, however the icons did not appear. I was unable to debug it, due to a lack of time. This feature could be included in a future release.




S7. Wineglass animation to show legal BAC content when country is selected

Due to the complexity of pure CSS animations and a lack of tutorials/examples I was unable to create such an animation. While I did find examples of glasses that fill up when hovered over, I did not have time to try and edit the CSS. I was unable to control the level of liquid in the glass through the data from my data.json. This feature could be included in a future release.




S8. Animation of car driving from left to right at top of webpage

This feature was included in my website, however I decided that it would be more suitable as a landing page than a top bar animation.




S15. Buttons/dots of right side of page to help scrolling

This feature was not necessary as my pages were not long enough to require scrolling.
