Design Specifications

In my research of websites displaying WHO Sustainable Development Goal 3 data, most of the websites were very well designed with graphs and button to control the data displayed on the site. However, the websites all had a few cons that can be improved upon by my product. Some websites have side corner pockets with distracting social media links and clunky scrolling, while others have too much data leading to a cluttered site or not enough ways to search through the data.
My product will solve these issues by have smoother scrolling by ensure that the site does not have redundant code that makes loading slower as well as not having too much data to overwhelm the user. In addition, the site will have an easy way to search for countries through a search bar that has auto-complete. Finally no social media link for promotions will be displayed on my site.
Finally, my site will build off the well designed functions and features of the 5 researched sites and will use aspects described in my Design Brief. My site will include animations to create a more visually entertaining experience.

S1. Non-clashing colour theme (preferably blues and greens and white and black)
S2. Hover over plotted data on graph to show more detailed stats
S3. Link to WHO stats for users to view raw tabular data
S4. Will have a bar graph to display all listed countries and their legal BAC limits side by side to give quick visual representation
S5. Will have a slider labeled with the range of legal BAC limits to let the user explore the stats for different countries
S6. Clicking logo will bring you back to home page
S7. Wineglass animation to show legal BAC content when country is selected
S8. Animation of car driving from left to right at top of webpage
S9. Button changes colour when hovered over and rounded buttons
S10. Faster site loading time (no redundant code and no cluttered pages)
S11. Easy to read text and font (Roboto, pt 12 and up)
S12. Dropdown of countries
S13. Entry box for typing country with auto-complete
S14. Text sentence of what the legal BAC content is
S15. Buttons/dots of right side of page to help scrolling
